Kim Dami Profile

Kim Da-mi (김다미) is a South Korean actress .
She was born on April 9, 1995 in Paju, Gyeonggi-do, Korea.
Her height is 170cm, weight is secret kg and blood type is O.
Her educational background is Bongilcheon Elementary School,
Bongilcheon Middle School, Bongilcheon High School.
She said to have dreamed of becoming an actor since she was young,
and it was good to feel the emotions of the characters while watching the drama.
After she became a high school student, she went to an acting academy.
And she entered Incheon National University Department of Performing Arts (graduated).
After entering the University, she also served as a fitting model for various clothing brands,
taking advantage of her height of 170 cm.
She also appeared in several commercials during her school years.
Entertainment is ANDu Entertainment (ANDMARQ).

And finally, in 2017, the independent film,
'Two Person At The Same Town Se1' Project, debuted in the film industry for the first time.
Then, 'Marionette', which was filmed at a similar time to the film, was released on April 19, 2018.
And the 'The Witch: Part 1. The Subversion',
who made her name known to the world in earnest, was released on June 27, 2018.
She was chosen by director Park Hoon-jung after overcoming a whocpping 1,500:1 ompetition rate.
Kim Da-mi quickly emerged as a 'monster rookie' as she played the main character Ko Ja-yoon in the film.
Her role as Ko Ja-yoon swept the rookie awards at various film festivals that year.
With her brilliant debut, attention was also focused on what her next film would be,
and the next one was originally known as the 'The Witch 2'.
However, the production of 'The Witch 2' had been delayed, making her next work uncertain.
Then, she was cast as the heroine of Itaewon Class,
which produces webtoons dramatized by JTBC, and her next film was confirmed as Itaewon Class.
Itaewon Class's ratings surpassed 10% nationwide in five episodes, making a hit from the first drama.
